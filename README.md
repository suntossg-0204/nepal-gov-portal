## 📖 About

**Nepal Sarkar Portal** is a free, open, and bilingual (English & Nepali) web directory that brings together **all official Government of Nepal (.gov.np) websites** in one easy-to-use place.

Whether you are a citizen looking for a government service, a student doing research, or a professional needing official information — this portal saves you time by giving you instant access to every government website organized by category.

> 🗓️ Displays live Nepal time (NPT) in both **Gregorian** and **Bikram Sambat (BS)** calendar formats.

## 🗂️ Website Categories

The portal organizes government websites into **9 categories**:

1. 🏛️ **Main Government** — President, PM Office, Parliament, Supreme Court, Election Commission
2. 📋 **Ministries** — All 19 federal ministries
3. 💹 **Finance & Economy** — Nepal Rastra Bank, IRD, Customs, NEPSE, SEBON
4. 🛡️ **Security & Law** — Nepal Police, Army, Armed Police, CIAA, Immigration
5. 🏥 **Health & Education** — Dept. of Health, Universities, NEB, CTEVT
6. 🚧 **Infrastructure & Services** — NEA, Nepal Telecom, Roads Dept., Nepal Airlines
7. 🏢 **Commissions & Constitutional Bodies** — PSC, NHRC, Law Commission, Press Council
8. 🗺️ **Provincial & Local Governments** — All 7 provinces with municipalities
9. ⚙️ **Other Services** — Passport, Foreign Employment, Drug Administration, NagrikApp

## 🚀 Getting Started

### View Locally

1. **Clone the repository:**
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/suntossg-0204/nepal-gov-portal.git
   ```

2. **Open in browser:**
   ```bash
   cd nepal-gov-portal
   open index.html
   ```
   Or simply double-click `index.html` to open it in your browser.

### Deploy to GitHub Pages

1. Fork this repository
2. Go to **Settings → Pages**
3. Set source to **main branch**
4. Your site will be live at `https://yourusername.github.io/nepal-gov-portal/`
